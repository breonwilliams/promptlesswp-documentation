## Activating Your License

### First-Time Installation
When you first install Promptless WP:

1. **Activation Prompt** appears automatically
2. **Choose your plan**:
   - Click "Skip" for free version (2-page limit)
   - Click "Activate License" for premium
3. **Enter license key** (premium only)
4. **Verify activation** - Check for success message

### Upgrading from Free to Premium

1. **Go to** WordPress Admin → Promptless WP
2. **Click** "Upgrade to Premium" button
3. **Purchase** a license from our website
4. **Enter** your license key
5. **Click** "Activate License"
6. **Enjoy** unlimited pages immediately!

## Managing Your License

### Check License Status
- **Location**: WordPress Admin → Promptless WP → License
- **Information shown**:
  - Current plan (Free/Premium)
  - Pages used vs limit
  - License expiration (if premium)
  - Activation status

### Transfer License
To move your license to a different site:

1. **Deactivate** on current site
2. **Go to** new WordPress site
3. **Install** Promptless WP
4. **Activate** with same license key

**Note**: Each license can only be active on one site at a time.

### Renew License
Premium licenses are annual subscriptions:

- **Auto-renewal** enabled by default
- **Email reminders** sent before expiration
- **Grace period** of 14 days after expiration
- **Functionality continues** during grace period

## Page Limits Explained

### How Page Counting Works
- **Counted**: Any page/post with Promptless WP sections enabled
- **Not counted**: Pages without our sections
- **Not counted**: Drafts and private pages
- **Reusable**: Delete old pages to free up slots (free plan)

### Reaching Your Limit (Free Plan)
When you reach 2 pages on the free plan:

1. **Warning appears** when creating 2nd page
2. **"Upgrade" prompt** when trying to create 3rd page
3. **Existing pages** continue working normally
4. **Can edit** existing pages without restriction
5. **Can delete** pages to make room for new ones

### No Limits (Premium Plan)
With premium license:
- Create unlimited pages
- No warning messages
- No restrictions
- Full freedom to build

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try before buying?
Yes! The free version lets you create 2 full pages to test all features before upgrading.

### What happens if my license expires?
- **Existing pages** continue displaying normally
- **Can edit** current pages
- **Cannot create** new pages beyond free limit
- **14-day grace period** to renew

### Can I use one license on multiple sites?
No, each license is for one site. We offer multi-site packages for agencies.

### Do I lose my content if I downgrade?
No! All your content remains intact. You just can't create new pages beyond the free limit.

### Is the free version limited in features?
The main limitation is 2 pages maximum. All section types and editing features work the same in free and premium versions.
